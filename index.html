Privacy by architecture

Your data never leaves your machine.

There's no account and no upload. When you drop your archive, Freeroam unzips and parses every GPS track right here in the browser tab. Nothing is sent to a server, because there is no server.

Export from Strava

Settings → My Account → Download or Delete Your Account → request your archive. Strava emails you a ZIP.

Drop it onto the page

Drag the ZIP straight onto Freeroam. No sign-up screen, no email required, no "connect your account" step.
